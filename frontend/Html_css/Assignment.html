What is Skill Swap?
Skill Swap is a peer-to-peer platform that allows students to exchange skills without monetary transactions. Students can offer skills they are good at and learn new skills from others in return.

Why Skill Swap?

  • Promotes collaborative learning
  • Helps students save money
  • Builds confidence and communication
  • Encourages peer mentorship

The Skill Swap Platform is a web-based system designed to facilitate peer-to-peer skill exchange among students without involving monetary transactions. The platform encourages collaborative learning, knowledge sharing, and community building using Google Sites and Google Workspace tools.

Objectives:

  • To promote skill-based learning among students
  • To provide a cost-free knowledge exchange platform
  • To build a collaborative student community

Skill Swap Request

The skill swapping process enables students to exchange skills without monetary involvement. A student creates a profile listing the skills they can offer and the skills they want to learn. Other students can browse these profiles and send a skill swap request when they find a suitable match. Once both students agree, they coordinate and exchange skills through mutual learning sessions.
Example:
Student A offers Mathematics tutoring and wants to learn Graphic Design.
Student B offers Graphic Design and wants help with Mathematics.

Student A sends a skill swap request to Student B. After accepting the request, both students teach each other their respective skills, completing a successful skill exchange.
